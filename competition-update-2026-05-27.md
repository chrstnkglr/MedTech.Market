## Spectral AI Receives FDA De Novo Clearance for DeepView® System for Burn Indication

**Author:** Spectral AI, Inc.  
**Source:** [GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/05/26/3300941/0/en/spectral-ai-receives-fda-de-novo-clearance-for-deepview-system-for-burn-indication.html)  
**Publication date:** May 26, 2026, 07:00 ET

Spectral AI announced that the U.S. FDA granted De Novo Classification for its DeepView System, authorizing commercial distribution in the United States. DeepView combines multispectral imaging with a proprietary AI algorithm to assess burn wound healing potential and support earlier treatment decisions in burn centers, trauma centers and emergency departments. The company states that image acquisition takes 0.2 seconds, with processing and AI classification taking approximately 20 to 25 seconds, based on a clinically validated burn wound image database.

The development is competition-relevant because it extends regulated imaging AI into acute, non-radiology settings where decisions can affect treatment pathways, length of stay and resource utilization. While not directly competing with CT, MR or X-ray portfolios, the clearance is a signal that imaging-derived AI diagnostics are moving into broader procedural and emergency workflows, potentially creating new expectations for rapid, embedded decision support at the point of care.

**Strategic relevance:** Trend indicator / Observation. The clearance supports the trend toward AI-enabled imaging devices that combine acquisition, analytics and workflow decision support in one regulated product, with value propositions centered on speed, standardization and cost efficiency.
